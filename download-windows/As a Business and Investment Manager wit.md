As a Business and Investment Manager with Scriptbank this is what is expected of you?

1. You are expected to Manage businesses using Scriptbank as their Payment Processing System or for the Point Of Sale
2. Scriptbank may Invest in these businesses, if that happens you are to ensure the investment is handled properly by the Business
3. You are to ensure the business is actively using Scriptbank as their primary payment Processing Platform
4. You are to manage the business Investment Portfolio with Scriptbank
5. You are to also get more Business Prospect for the Company
